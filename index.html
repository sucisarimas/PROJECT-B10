General English

The foundation of the language that focuses on basic grammar and vocabulary in developing the four skills of speaking, reading, writing and listening on a wide range of topics.

Bussiness English

The type of English used in business contexts, such as international trade, commerce, finance, insurance, banking, and many office settings.

For Schools

Studying English extensively such as how to pronounce English, English vocabulary, how to write in English, English grammar, English conversation and others.

Young Learners

English for young learners needs to obtain communication skills rather than knowledge about English, includes all language competencies in the form of listening, speaking, reading and writing skills.
